ABOOUT US

Making real impact on the ecological footprint crisis by connecting people and farmers.

LocalEat is a platform that helps people minimize their ecological footprint. The main focus is to find local produce for people;shipping produce is an unnecessary act that causes a lot of pollution, which contributes to the ecological footprint. Other than offering the user services that help them find a nearby farm, or local food manufacture that they can buy from, there are also tips on how people can change their daily habits, in order to minimize their ecological footprint.
